FAQ

How do you play?

If you have not registered yet, you need to have an account to play

You start off with 100 coins that you can bet on streamers

You need to enter the amount you want to bet, select a button, and click confirm bet

Betting is available after the streamer as either won or lost, waiting for a new game to start

I won a game, why dont my balance update?

In order to update your balance, you need to manually refresh the site

What can you do with your coins?

At the moment you can't do anything with your coins, but soon you will be able to withdraw into real cash

I lost all my coins, how do i get more?

You can request free coins at our navigation bar. Free Coins will show up in your account within 24 hours
